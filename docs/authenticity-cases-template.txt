Authenticity Tracker (Simple)

Goal
- Check if reviewer is genuine.
- Check if review text is genuine.
- Use score to decide: Genuine, Borderline, or High Risk.

Scoring
- Weight: 1 to 10 (importance)
- Result:
  - PASS = 1
  - BORDERLINE = 0.5
  - FAIL = 0
- Points = Weight x Result
- Final % = (Total Points / Total Weight) x 100

Decision
- 80% and above: Likely Genuine
- 60% to 79%: Borderline (manual review)
- Below 60%: High Risk

Main Inputs
- login_method (GOOGLE or LINKEDIN)
- reviewer_name
- reviewer_title
- reviewer_role
- reviewer_company_name
- reviewer_email_domain (if available)
- linkedin_profile_url (if LinkedIn login)
- review_text
